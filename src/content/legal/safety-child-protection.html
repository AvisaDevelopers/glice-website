01Child safety at Glice

Glice is committed to a safe, respectful environment using our live video chat and social discovery platform. Child protection is a core responsibility, and we enforce strict safeguards aligned with recognized global safety expectations.

02Glice standards against CSAE

We maintain a zero-tolerance approach to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Our team works to prevent abuse, detect harmful behavior quickly, and report credible risks to relevant authorities.

03Commitment to child protection

Glice actively monitors for CSAE-related risks and takes immediate action on harmful conduct, suspicious profiles, or abusive content.

04Zero tolerance for CSAM and exploitation

Glice strictly forbids all of the following:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
  • Grooming, enticement, or solicitation of minors.
  • Sexual or otherwise inappropriate communication with minors.
  • Uploading, sharing, storing, or forwarding CSAM in any format.
  • Any use of the platform for trafficking or exploitation.

Accounts involved in this activity are permanently removed and escalated to law enforcement.

05Detection, reporting, and rapid review

Users can report profiles and messages through in-app safety tools. Every child-safety report is prioritized for urgent moderation review and enforcement.

06Legal and regulatory compliance

Glice follows applicable child-protection frameworks across regions, including:

  • Canada: Online Harms Act (Bill C-63) and Bill S-210.
  • United States: COPPA requirements where applicable.
  • United Kingdom: Online Safety Act obligations.
  • European Union: GDPR protections related to minors.
  • Other local and international child safety laws as required.

07User rules and enforcement

  • Glice is for adults aged 18+ only.
  • Any suspected underage account is removed after verification review.
  • Users linked to CSAE activity are permanently banned and reported.

08Child safety contact

Parents, guardians, agencies, and community members can contact our Child Safety Team directly:

  • Email: childsafety@glice.app

09Ongoing safety commitment

We regularly review our tools, policies, and moderation processes to strengthen protection efforts and improve response quality over time.

10Community and inclusivity

Glice welcomes adults from all backgrounds and cultures. We also prioritize accessibility for users with disabilities through inclusive design and profile preference options.
