1Respect comes first

Treat others with courtesy in video calls, chat, and profile interactions. Harassment, hate speech, intimidation, or repeated unwanted contact is not allowed.

2Adults only platform

Glice is intended for users aged 18 and above. Any account suspected to be underage may be reviewed and removed.

3Be authentic

Use your own identity and accurate profile details. Misleading impersonation, fake profiles, or deceptive behavior can lead to enforcement actions.

4Prohibited content and conduct

  • Nudity, sexual exploitation, and explicit abuse content.
  • Violent, graphic, or threatening material.
  • Illegal activity, trafficking, scams, or extortion.
  • Spam, malware links, and coordinated abuse.

5Consent-based messaging

Chat access is unlocked through mutual interest. Respect boundaries at every step and stop communication when requested.

6Safety tools and reporting

If behavior feels unsafe, use block and report immediately. Our moderation workflow prioritizes user safety and policy violations.

7Enforcement

Violations may result in warning, feature restrictions, suspension, or permanent account removal depending on severity.

8Guideline updates

We may revise these guidelines as the platform evolves. Continued use of Glice means you agree to the latest version.
